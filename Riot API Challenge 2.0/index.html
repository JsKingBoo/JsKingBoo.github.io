How to play:

  • The aim of the game is to match every champion with the brawler she, he, or it bought.
  • Cycle through guess choices by clicking on the brawler icons above.
  • The brawlers' cycle is Razorfin >> Ironback >> Plundercrab >> Ocklepod.
  • Submit your guess by clicking "Submit Guess". Clear your guess to default by clicking on "Clear Guess".
  • After you guess, the number of correct, misplaced, and incorrect brawlers will appear. Use this information to make your guesses more accurate.
