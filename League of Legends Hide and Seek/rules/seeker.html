Seeker Rules for League of Legends Hide and Seek

  • Seekers may not leave their base until 2 minutes into the game (2:00)
  • Only one (1) Rylai's Crystal Scepter and one (1) Frozen Maller are allowed per team. The second isn't allowed until 25 minutes
  • Oracles, Lightbringer, and Hextech Sweeper are banned until 15 minutes
  • Seekers must return to base at 300 HP or below. Failure to do so allows hiders to kill you
  • Seekers may have AT MOST one (1) Clairvoyance summoner spell
