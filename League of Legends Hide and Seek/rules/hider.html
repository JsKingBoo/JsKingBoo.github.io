Hider Rules for League of Legends Hide and Seek

  • Hiders may not use an ability unless it provides a form of crowd control, mobility, shielding, healing, and/or damage mitigation. Skills that provide VISION and/or DAMAGE ONLY are NOT allowed at any point. Combinations of damage-only abilities to generate CC (such as Brand stun) is also not allowed
  • The group and run tactic is not allowed
    • Hiders may not hide together in groups of three (3) or larger
    • A third hider is not allowed to join two (2) hiders even while being chased
    • A chased hider may only receive assistance from AT MOST ONE (1) other hider
  • Any item which doesn't give movement, CC, or defensive stats are banned for hiders
  • Hiders may not auto attack, even to proc lifesteal, unless it is to proc CC (such as Udyr's bear stance)
  • Hiders may not kill a seeker at 300 HP or under UNLESS the seeker does not attempt to back for three (3) seconds
  • Hiders may not interrupt a seeker attempting to base
  • Hiders may not be in base unless they die
  • Hiders may buy when dead
  • Respawned hiders get a 30 second grace period
  • Hiders who arrive, remain in, or return to base not during the grace period are huntable and lose a life
  • Rylai's Crystal Scepter does not legitimize nor justify a spell
  • Hiders are "out" when they have 0 lives left
  • Hiders who are "out" may not affect any hider who is currently not "out"
  • "Out" hiders may never affect seekers
