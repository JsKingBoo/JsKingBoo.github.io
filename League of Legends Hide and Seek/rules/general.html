General Rules for League of Legends Hide and Seek

  • The objective of the game is for the seekers to kill every hider a certain amount of times
  • Hiders have five (5) lives
  • Games last forty (40) minutes
  • The game is played on Dominion in draft mode
  • Capping points is not allowed. Greater Relics (the thingies in the middle of the map) are fine
